Shaping

Our experienced carpenters shape and make artworks in strict compliance with the drawings and templates as provided by the designers. With greatest care, dedication and state-of-the-art workmanship, the timber is manually molded and assembled by the traditional mortise joint for stability and tightness. Then through times of elaborate and scrupulous modification, the works are crafted up to the requirements of designing and process. The mortise joint itself can be a classic and impressive touch. In addition to its natural grains, Minaxdo wooden artworks distinguish themselves with strikingly charming vision.
