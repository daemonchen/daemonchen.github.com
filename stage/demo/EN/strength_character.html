  • [Shanshui Cabinet]

    The surface of the cabinet is derived from a Chinese Landscape painting Qian li Jiang Shan, which expresses the rise and fall of the mountains with the concave-convex of the palings. The four doors can be opened and closed ingeniously. It can be placed either in the sitting room or the bedroom just like a landscape picture painted with wood as pen and paper.
