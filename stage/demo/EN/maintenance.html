Maintenance Service

We provide two years of free maintenance service. Every 90 days, you can send the wooden artworks to the factory for cleaning, polishing, waxing, and other various maintenance items to be carried out by professionals. In addition, you can use the professional maintenance kit to carry out routine maintenance on the wooden artworks by yourself from time to time.

Maintenance Tips

*Please avoid direct sunlight as far as possible in order to avoid surface oxidation.

*Avoid using hot water, disinfectant, or alkaline liquid to wipe the timber, so as to prevent the coating on the wooden artworks surface from being damaged.

*The use of clean and dry duster, soft cloth or other gentle material is recommended to remove any dust or dirt adhered to the surface of the wooden artworks.

*Regularly wax and polish the product to make it resistant to moister and shiny.

*In spring and summer when moisture is heavy indoors, please pad the wooden artworks and maintain a gap of 0.5 to 1 centimeter between it and the wall, to prevent the wood from being affected by moisture and resulting in expansion.

*Do not put the product at hot, dry places so as to prevent the wood from cracking and deformation. In autumn and in winter the product should be placed about 1 meter away from the heating device so as to avoid the wood from being chapped and deformation.

*If you have children in your house, it is recommended that you carefully consider the placement of the wooden artworks so as to prevent children from getting injured when playing.

*Please pay attention to the load-bearing range of the wooden artworks.
