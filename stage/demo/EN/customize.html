Customization Service

All single products of Minaxdo Wooden Artworks are customized. Valuable and finest original timber as well as exquisite accessories materials are selected according to the needs of the customers before senior craftsmen carry out elaborate fabrication for a few months (at least 60 days), paying attention to the artful decorations and delicate details to demonstrate high quality design and production in contemporary China, rendering the product high artistic value and collection value.
